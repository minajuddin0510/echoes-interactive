## 📖 About

**ECHOES** is an immersive, scroll-driven interactive storytelling experience that takes users on a journey through five defining moments in human history — from the discovery of fire 2 million years ago, to the present day.

Rather than presenting history as dry facts and dates, ECHOES treats it as a living narrative — asking one question throughout:

> *What echoes did we leave behind — and what will yours be?*

The final chapter invites users to write their own one-sentence echo and add it to a living wall of responses, turning passive readers into active participants in the human story.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔥 **5 immersive chapters** — Fire, Language, Cities, Print, Now
- 📜 **Scroll-triggered animations** — GSAP ScrollTrigger with parallax depth
- 🎭 **Cinematic typography** — Playfair Display editorial design
- 💬 **Interactive Echo Wall** — users leave their own message for the future
- 🧭 **Chapter dot navigation** — fixed side indicator with smooth scroll
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — desktop, tablet, and mobile
- ⚡ **Zero dependencies** — no frameworks, no API, pure frontend
